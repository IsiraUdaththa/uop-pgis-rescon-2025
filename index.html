About

The PGIS Research Congress is the major annual research conference of the Postgraduate Institute of Science. The congress provides an excellent platform to share knowledge and network with scientists across the country and abroad through the presentation of cutting-edge research.

The Congress will be held on the 7th and 8th of November 2025. The Inauguration Ceremony, together with the keynote speech by a distinguished scientist, will be held on the 7th of November.

Technical Sessions of RESCON 2025 will be held on 7th, and 8th of November at the PGIS, and international participants will attend online.

Main RESCON 2025 Conference

I warmly welcome you to RESCON2025, the flagship research congress of PGIS, to be held on 7 November 2025.

With the theme “Synergy in Science: Uniting Disciplines for a Better Tomorrow,” this event reinforces our commitment to interdisciplinary research and meaningful collaboration.

Featuring leading scientists and over 400 participants, RESCON2025 is a vital platform for sharing ideas, driving innovation, and building a stronger, more resilient Sri Lanka.

It is my honor to welcome you to RESCON 2025, a platform celebrating scientific discovery, collaboration, and innovation. This year's theme, “Synergy in Science: Uniting Disciplines for a Better Tomorrow,” highlights the power of interdisciplinary work in addressing today's global challenges.

With over 400 postgraduate participants and contributions across fields—from AI and ICT to Life, Earth, and Physical Sciences—RESCON 2025 promises inspiring talks, presentations, and opportunities for networking and collaboration.

I encourage you to share ideas, challenge perspectives, and build connections that spark progress beyond this congress. Together, let's embrace the spirit of synergy and shape a better future through science.

Themes

The conference covers a wide range of scientific and interdisciplinary topics.

AI in Natural Sciences / Industrial Aspects

Applications of artificial intelligence in scientific research and industrial development.

Earth & Environmental Sciences

Studies on climate, ecosystems, geology, and sustainable environmental practices.

ICT, Mathematics & Statistics

Advances in computing, data analysis, and mathematical modeling.

Life Sciences

Research in biology, medicine, genetics, and related fields.

Science Education

Innovative methods and practices in science education.

Physical Sciences

Topics in physics, chemistry, and materials science.

Abstract Submission Guidelines

Abstract submission will start on the 10th April of 2025 and end on 30th June of 2025. Notification of Acceptance will be sent on 30th July of 2025 .

Please read the General Guidelines carefully, and make sure to use the templates provided for both the abstract and the extended abstract. We are committed to hosting the conference in-person and requests for online presentations will be considered under special circumstances, particularly for international presenters.

Please read the abstract submission guidelines below. Since we get more than 200 abstracts for each RESCON, we want to streamline the process so that it is easier for the reviewers.

For RESCON 2024, we will process the submitted abstract and make sure they are ready for review process, this will be done by our editorial assistants.
