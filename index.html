RESCON 2025

The Premier Postgraduate Research Congress of Sri Lanka.

Postgraduate Institute of Science. University of Peradeniya

November 7-8, 2025

About

The PGIS Research Congress is the major annual research conference of the Postgraduate Institute of Science. The congress provides an excellent platform to share knowledge and network with scientists across the country and abroad through the presentation of cutting-edge research.


The Congress will be held on the 7th, and 8th of November 2025. The Inauguration Ceremony, together with the keynote speech by a distinguished scientist, will be held on the 7thof November.


Technical Sessions of RESCON 2025 will be held on 7th, and 8th of November at the PGIS, and international participants will attend online.

Abstract Submission Guidelines

Abstract submission will start on the 10th April of 2025 and end on 10th June of 2025. Notification of Acceptance will be sent on 30th July of 2025 .


Please read the General Guidelines carefully, and make sure to use the templates provided for both the abstract and the extended abstract. We are committed to hosting the conference in-person and requests for online presentations will be considered under special circumstances, particularly for international presenters.

General Guidelines

Please read the abstract submission guidelines below. Since we get more than 200 abstracts for each RESCON, we want to streamline the process so that it is easier for the reviewers.


For RESCON 2024, we will process the submitted abstract and make sure they are ready for review process, this will be done by our editorial assistants.

Templates

Kindly use the following two templates when you write your abstract and extended abstract


If you do not use the templates, the editorial assistants will contact you and help you put the document to the template.

Submit a Abstract

Before you submit the abstract, be sure that

You have the abstract with you as an MS Word document prepared according to the template.

You have the extended abstract with you as an MS Word document prepared according to the template.
